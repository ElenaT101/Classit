Our mission

CLASSIT inc is a web-based tool that allows students, teachers, and parents to network efficiently. CLASSIT inc platform will enable teachers to easily communicate with their students and parents. Capabilities include assigning digital passes, homework, classwork, and exams. Outside of school, students will be able to use a public forum to ask others for help on assignments, set study focus timers, and manage their schedules.
